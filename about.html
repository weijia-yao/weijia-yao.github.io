Weijia Yao – About Me

DTPA, University of Groningen

Weijia Yao is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in systems and control theory with the Engineering and Technology Institute (ENTEG), University of Groningen, Groningen, the Netherlands. His research interests include nonlinear systems and control, robotics and multiagent systems. Mr. Yao was a finalist for the Best Conference Paper Award at ICRA in 2021, and the recipient of the Outstanding Master Degree Dissertation award of Hunan province, China, in 2020.
